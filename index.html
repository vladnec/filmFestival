About

We love films

Watch it outside Boston Edition is one of the biggest events in the city hosted at the Boston Public Garden you will get the best experience in the open space. We are glad to present 4 days of amazing movies from all over the world.

We bring film culture to our community

Our Epic event will take place on the beautiful city of Boston from the 5th to the 8th of August from 6:00pm to midnight. Clara Dunn, Co founder of Watch it outside events Co. personally invites you to this magnificient venue, FREE OF CHARGE ! Just make sure to make a reservation for you and your family/friends.
